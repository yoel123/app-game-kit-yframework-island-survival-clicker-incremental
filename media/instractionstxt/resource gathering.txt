to gather resources click on collect resources in the main game screen.
pick a location you want to try gathering resources from, collecting resources costs points,
5 points for woods and for the rest its 10 points per click.
certin tools will help you collect more resources for example:
an axe will help you chop more wood at the forrest, a pickaxe will help you mine more ore
in the mine, a shovel will help you gather sand on the beach.
bewere as you waltz around you may attract the attention of zombies that will disrupt your efforts
and instead of collecting resources you will run for your life, in small numbers they are not a threat.
but in large numbers you will have to fight them to be able to collect resourses safely.
also while collecting resources your tools might break and you will need to craft new ones.
bronze tools has less chance of breacking
